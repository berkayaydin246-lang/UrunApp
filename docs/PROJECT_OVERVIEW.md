# Project Overview

## Problem
People in Turkey often cannot understand the ingredients listed on packaged food products. They see terms like sodium nitrite, sodium polyphosphate, glucose syrup, palm oil, stabilizers, preservatives, and emulsifiers but do not know what they mean.

## Target User
Primary users:
- 35–65 age group
- Parents and families
- People who want simple, clear food label explanations
- Users who do not want technical nutrition language

## Product Promise
"Ürünü ara veya tara, içinde ne olduğunu sade Türkçeyle öğren."

## Core Value
The app helps users understand:
- What ingredients mean
- Which additives require attention
- Whether a product should be consumed carefully
- Whether the product is highly processed

## What This App Is Not
- It is not a medical app.
- It is not a dietitian replacement.
- It does not diagnose or treat health conditions.
- It does not make absolute claims like "this is harmful" without rule-based evidence.
